About Me

Hi, My name is Daniel. For all 16 years of my life, I've lived in Oklahoma with a passion for figuring out how things work.

Around the time I turned 6, I learned about these things called computers that could show practically anything I wanted to know.

Being a curious 6-year-old, I thought that was really cool and I wanted to know how this worked, but of course I didn't understand what code was back then and I wouldn't know for several years.

When I was about 12 years old, I learned about this cool thing called HTML that would let me make websites. I began taking classes learning of the existence of programming languages like Python, C, and Java.

When 2020 hit, my knowledge of how to code grew exponentially and this was the first time I tried actually making a website.

It was pretty terrible, but in the 4 years since, I've learned a lot about crucial web design elements
